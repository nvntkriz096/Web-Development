ExO Work Welcomes

VICE CHAIRMAN

TONY SALDANHA

New York, December 21, 2018 -ExO Works, the advisory firm which helps companies execute innovation using the Exponential Organizations methodology, is excited to welcome "Tony Saldanha as Vice Chairman of the company

As Vice Chairman, Tony Saldanha's role will focus on assisting the leadership team of ExO Works and their clients to develop innovation strategies and roadmaps that complement ExO Workss core offerings. This addition to the ExO Works team aligns with the companyss mission to be an innovation partner for clients, focus on long-term relationships, and shift organizations to adopt exponential behaviors and mindset. This new approach also supports ExO Workss continued development of the premium "ExO Sprint process.

"Tony was one of the first adopters of the ExO Sprint. He had the same frustrations, aspirations, and needs our Sprint clients are coming to us to address. That experience, coupled with his aptitude for championing new models, will help us achieve 10x results with these Sprints," said Salim Ismail, Co-Founder & Chairman of ExO Works.
